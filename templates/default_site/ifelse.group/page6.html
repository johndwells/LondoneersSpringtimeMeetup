Advanced Conditionals as Tag Parameters

Before

{exp:channel:entries
	limit="3"
	status="{if get:status}{get:status}{if:else}open{/if}"}
	{title} - {status}<br>
{/exp:channel:entries}

After

{exp:channel:entries
	limit="3"
	parse="inward"
	status="{exp:ifelse}{if get:status}{get:status}{if:else}open{/if}{/exp:ifelse}"}
	{title} - {status}<br>
{/exp:channel:entries}
